Compensating Factor Logic

V4.0 introduces compensating factor analysis - the ability for strengths in one area to partially offset weaknesses in another. This nuanced approach prevents false denials while maintaining risk discipline.

Example 1: Low CFA + Strong Income

Base Metrics:
  • CFA: 55% (below Tier 2 threshold of 60%)
  • Income Stability: 95/100 (excellent)
  • Multiple income sources: Payroll + 2 gig platforms
  • Base CFRS: 58
Compensating Logic:

Exceptional income stability (+3 points) + income diversity bonus (+2 points) compensates for modest CFA.

Adjusted CFRS: 58 + 5 = 63

Decision: Tier 2 - APPROVED

Previously would have been denied (CFRS 58 < 60 threshold). Now approved with $300-$500 loan.

Example 2: Thin File + Excellent Behavior

Base Metrics:
  • Transaction History: 2 months (thin file)
  • Behavior Score: 98/100 (perfect - zero NSF, no overdrafts)
  • CFA: 78% (good)
  • Balance Trend: +20% MoM (strong growth)
  • Base CFRS: 47
Compensating Logic:

Perfect behavior (+2 points) + strong balance trend (+5 points) compensates for limited history.

Adjusted CFRS: 47 + 7 = 54

Decision: Tier 2 - APPROVED

Previously auto-denied for thin file. Now approved with $200-$400 loan and 60-day graduation review.
